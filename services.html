Statutory Audits

The Firm’s strength lies in being able to conducts all kinds of audits for large & medium sized business entities, prepare the financial statements, in accordance with Accounting Standards, auditing standards and latest auditing techniques.​
Our service in auditing includes:

  • Audit of public and private limited companies under The Companies Act, 2013.
  • Audit of partnership firms, and proprietary concern.
  • Audit of Banks and Insurance Companies.
  • Audit of Non-profit organization.
  • Tax audit under The Income Tax Act, 1961 for all entities.
